About

Empowering farmers through innovative solutions for sustainable agriculture.

Our Mission

We aim to revolutionize agriculture by providing technology-driven solutions for plant disease detection. Our system leverages Deep Learning to help farmers identify crop diseases early and take timely actions, improving productivity and reducing losses.


What We Do

Our platform uses Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to classify diseases in tomato and potato crops. Farmers can upload leaf images to our application, which provides accurate disease classifications along with actionable solutions.

Our Impact

We provide farmers with an advanced tool to detect plant diseases early, ensuring timely action to protect their crops. In addition, we connect farmers to local pesticide dealers, offering tailored solutions for their specific agricultural needs. This integrated approach minimizes losses and enhances productivity.
